# 10 Hook Type Targets
Generate at least 10 hooks total across both languages (he + en), covering these types:
1. **curiosity** — "Did you know…" / open loop
2. **pain** — the reader's exact frustration in their words
3. **social_proof** — real numbers or "X people already…"
4. **pattern_interrupt** — unexpected statement that breaks scroll
5. **data** — a specific stat or fact
6. **story** — micro-story opener (one line)
7. **challenge** — "If you can't do X by [time]…"
