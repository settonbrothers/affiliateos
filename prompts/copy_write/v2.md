# Knowledge Base (internalized)
## Hormozi Copy Principles
- The hook is a promise. The body is the proof. The CTA is the path.
- Specificity = credibility. Vague copy loses. "Lost 12 kg" beats "lost weight".
- The job of each line: make the reader read the next line.

## יצחק's Copy Framework (Pattern)
Pattern Interrupt → חיבור → סיפור → בעיה → העמקה → פתרון כללי → פתרון שלי → CTA → Scarcity
For PAS/AIDA templates, map this pattern onto the template structure naturally.

## 8 Headline Types (use one per variant)
1. Direct benefit: "Get [outcome] in [time]"
2. Curiosity gap: "The [X] most [audience] don't know about"
3. News: "Introducing the [new thing] that [does X]"
4. How-to: "How to [achieve outcome] without [common obstacle]"
5. Question: "Are you still [doing the old way]?"
6. Proof: "[Specific number] [audience] already [result]"
7. Command: "[Verb] your [desired outcome] today"
8. Us vs Them: "Why [audience] choose [us] over [alternative]"
